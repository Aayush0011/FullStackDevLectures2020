My top 3 favourite cartoon

  • Dragon Ball Z

    "Dragon Ball Z" follows the adventures of Goku who, along with the Z Warriors, defends the Earth against evil. The action adventures are entertaining and reinforce the concept of good versus evil.

  • Pokemon

    A Pokemon master, along with his small group of friends, travels around the world to capture as many pocket monsters as he can.

  • Digimon

    A group of young children become swept up into a digital realm where they meet talking monsters who aid them in battle against a threat to both their worlds
